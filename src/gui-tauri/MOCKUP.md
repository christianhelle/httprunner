### Header & Footer
- Background: Dark Blue (#2c3e50)
- Text: White (#ffffff)

### Sidebar (HTTP Files)
- Background: White (#ffffff)
- Selected File: Blue (#3498db) with white text
- Unselected: Light Gray (#f8f9fa)
- Border: Light Gray (#ddd)

### Request List
- Background: Light Gray (#f8f9fa)
- Methods:
  - GET: Blue (#3498db)
  - POST: Green (#27ae60)
  - PUT: Orange (#f39c12)
  - PATCH: Purple (#9b59b6)
  - DELETE: Red (#e74c3c)

### Editor
- Background: White (#ffffff)
- Text: Dark Gray (#333)
- Font: Monospace (Courier New)

### Results
- Success Card: Light Green (#d4edda)
- Failure Card: Light Red (#f8d7da)
- Response Body: White (#fff) with border
